📖 ChurnLens AI - User & Integration Guide

1. Overview

Welcome to ChurnLens AI, a premium predictive dashboard designed for analyzing and intervening in customer churn risk. The system evaluates 26 parameters computed from historical RFM, support tickets, and web behavioral snapshot telemetry to forecast whether a customer is likely to churn in the next 60 days.

2. Single Predictor Tab

Use this tab to evaluate individual customer records manually:

  • Persona Presets: Click one of the profiles (e.g. Active Promoter, Slipping Champion) to instantly auto-fill the forms with sample parameters and submit them.
  • Feature Inputs: Tweak variables across Demographics, RFM purchases, Customer Support, and Web Activity. Bubble labels show exact value numbers.
  • Results Gauge: The radial circular dial shows Churn Score probability. If it exceeds the threshold, the risk turns High Risk (Red), otherwise Low Risk (Green).
  • CRM Action Triggers: Shows concrete recommendations based on integration guides (e.g. suppressed ads, VIP Slack warnings for users with open tickets and spend > $1000).

3. Batch File Upload Tab

Designed for evaluating multiple user profiles simultaneously (e.g., nightly ETL CRM processing):

  • Drag-and-Drop: Drop a CSV file containing customer columns matching the schema features into the zone.
  • Overview KPI Cards: Aggregates metrics including total customers uploaded, average churn probability score, percentage flagged, and Estimated Revenue at Risk.
  • Predictions Table: Search by ID, filter by risk class, and export results back as a labeled CSV.

4. Model Insights & Simulation

Understand classifier characteristics and customize policies:

  • Threshold Slider: Dynamically change the boundary classification threshold (production default: 0.40) to instantly update labels on single and batch views.
  • Responsible AI Guidelines: Approved for WIN-BACK funnels and CRM tags; strictly prohibited for pricing discrimination or throttling service.

Responsible AI Use Guidelines

✅ Approved Use Cases
Route at-risk customers into automated CRM email funnels, deliver personalized win-back offers (such as 15% discount packages), and alert customer success agents to prioritize outstanding support tickets.
❌ Restricted Actions
Baseline pricing discrimination or gouging is strictly forbidden. The prediction model must never be used to degrade service speed, throttle bandwidth, or deny standard support channels.

Features Dictionary (26 Input Schema)

customer_id
Unique identifier for customer trace. Dropped during inference.
recency_days
Days since the customer's last purchase. Key indicator of loyalty.
frequency_180d
Number of unique purchases made in the last 180 days.
monetary_180d
Total spending of the customer during the last 180 days.
ticket_count_90d
Total support tickets submitted in the last 90 days.
negative_ticket_rate_90d
Ratio of support tickets marked with disappointed or negative sentiment.
avg_resolution_hours_90d
Average time (hours) support took to close tickets.
last_visit_days_ago
Days since the customer last logged onto the web interface.
sessions_30d
Web sessions logged in the last 30 days.
abandoned_carts_30d
Unsubmitted carts containing products in the last 30 days.
avg_discount_pct_180d
Average discount percentage applied to historical orders.
return_rate_180d
Percentage of purchased products returned by the user.
